The Super Nintendo

After the success of the NES nintendo needed a follow up to compete with the 16-bit Sega Genesis Their answer was the Super Nintendo. The SNES was much more powerful than the NES not only with only only with a 16 bit processor but also had a Super FX chip.What is a Super FX chip? The Super FX chip is what allowed the SNES to display 3D graphics in games such as Star Fox and parallax scrolling famously used in Super Castlevania. Parallax scrolling is where the background moves at a different speed than the foreground or even in a different pattern entirely. This gave the SNES a huge advantage in the hardware department. But what good is powerful hardware without good games, well the SNES has some of the best games of all time such as Earthbound, Starfox, Super Castlevania, Chrono Trigger, Super Mario RPG, Super Metroid, Super Mario World, and many more fantastic games. The SNES also improved on the NES controller by adding 2 more face buttons and 2 shoulder buttons.
