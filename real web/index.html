4 Classic Consoles

If someone says the words classic many thoughts will come to your head. For some people that thought will involve video games and no consoles describe classic as much the nintendo entertainment system, the super nintendo entertainment system, the atari 2600, and the Sega Genesis. All of which are classic consoles and set the standards for consoles to come. Nintendo, Sega, and Activision could even be considered the fathers of console gaming. While not making the first home consoles they made the first massively popular ones. Starting with the Atari, NES, Sega Genesis, and SNES in order of their release. Many people who have never even played video games could hear and recognize the name of some of the titles on these consoles such as, Super Mario Bros, Sonic The Hedgehog, Super Mario World, and Frogger.
